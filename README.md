# Apple x 1568 Broadway Times Square Partnership Proposal
Strategic partnership proposal exploring Apple as a potential naming rights partner for 1568 Broadway Times Square. Developed as part of an internal intern competition at IPG360. This project represents a hypothetical opportunity and is not affiliated with Apple or 1568 Broadway.

## Overview
This project analyzes how Apple’s brand identity and customer engagement strategy could translate into a high-visibility Times Square presence at 1568 Broadway.

The goal is to understand:
- How Apple uses high-traffic locations for brand visibility
- The strategic value of Times Square as an advertising space
- Key takeaways for marketing and brand positioning
- Potential activation opportunities for Apple

## Objectives
- Analyze the location opportunity and surrounding environment
- Evaluate brand visibility and messaging
- Identify strengths and weaknesses of the campaign
- Provide strategic recommendations

## Project Context
- This project was developed as part of an internal intern competition at IPG360. Interns were assigned a high-visibility location opportunity and tasked with identifying a company that could strategically utilize the space.
- The objective was to research how a potential partner connects with its customers, understand the different audiences and social communities associated with the brand, and develop a strategic partnership concept.
- This presentation was created for an internal audience of IPG executives and department leaders. While not commissioned by Apple or intended as a real-world proposal, the project was designed to simulate the strategic analysis and partnership development process that would precede an initial sponsorship conversation.
